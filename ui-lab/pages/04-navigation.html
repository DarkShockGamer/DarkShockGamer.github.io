Trident Robotics is a student-led FIRST Robotics Competition team. Founded in 2017, we bring together students passionate about STEM to design, build, and program competitive robots. We're committed to gracious professionalism both on and off the field.
The FRC competition season runs January through April each year. Build season kicks off in early January with the game reveal, followed by six weeks of intensive building. Regional and district events take place in March, with the World Championship in April.
Three easy steps:
1. Attend one of our open house nights in September.
2. Complete the interest form and submit your signed waiver.
3. Show up to kickoff in January ready to build!
Mentors are adult volunteers — engineers, programmers, teachers, and business professionals — who guide students without doing the work for them. They share expertise, run workshops, ensure safety, and help students navigate design decisions throughout the season.
