Build Season Timeline

Interactive timeline for planning and tracking build season activities

How to Use

  • Resize: Drag the handles on either end of bars to adjust their duration
  • Keyboard: Left/Right arrows resize from active handle (default: right), Shift+Arrow for faster, L/R keys to switch handles, Enter to edit labels, Escape to deselect
  • Auto-save: Changes are automatically saved to your browser
  • Export: Download your timeline as JSON data or PNG image
