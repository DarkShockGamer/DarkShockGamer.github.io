How to Use

  • Click a bar or team name to open the edit panel and adjust dates & color
  • Drag handles on either end of a bar to resize it directly
  • Keyboard: focus a bar, then press ←/→ (or Shift+←/→ for week jumps) to adjust the end date; Enter to open editor
  • Season dates set the overall view window; Zoom narrows it further
  • Auto-sync: every change is saved automatically and shared with all devices in real-time
  • Export / Import JSON to share or back up your timeline
  • The red line marks today's date
